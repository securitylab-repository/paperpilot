# Evidence-Centric Synthesis in Scientific Writing

## Objective
The objective of this study is to design a robust synthesis pipeline for literature-backed writing.

## Method
We employed a mixed-method protocol with n=640 documents across three domains.

## Results
Results show a significant increase in coherence and a decrease in unsupported statements.
Precision: 0.89. Recall: 0.84.

## Conclusion
In conclusion, the approach is effective for claim-grounded drafting.

## Limitations
A limitation is the language bias toward English-only corpora.
